About Me

I was born in Washington State and have lived there my entire life. I doubt I will ever grow tired of this beautiful region, nor could I ever hope to fully explore it. When I am not making maps, I enjoy hiking, kayaking, climbing, and making pixel art, a medium I have been working in since 2014.

BA in Environmental Sustainability | Magna cum laude

MS in Geospatial Technologies | In progress

What's Next?

I am currently enrolled as a Master's student in the Geospatial Technologies program at the University of Washington - Tacoma. Here, I am drawing upon my past work to model temporal changes in the accessibility of Washington's public lands.
